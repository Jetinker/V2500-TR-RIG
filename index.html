V2500 TR Rig Calculator

FOR REFERENCE USE ONLY

This calculator uses interpolation mapping to bring
any out of tolerance measurement ranging from
0 - 5.5mm (and inch equivalent) into the center of
the associated tolerance range. This calculator
WILL NOT compensate for loose standards!
ex. applying more/less pressure on calipers when
taking measurements. Not paying attention to the
"lip" that is created around the circumference of a
laminated shim after it is peeled. Using an amount
of Play-Doh greater than the area of your thumb nail
and about 6mm(0.25 inch) thick.
To ensure a single solution return to tolerance,
keep all measurement practices as uniform as possible.
